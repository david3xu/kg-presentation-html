Industry Application - Healthcare

Healthcare Knowledge Graph Applications:

  • Drug Discovery
    • Target identification through pathway analysis
    • Drug repurposing through similarity detection
    • Drug interaction prediction
  • Clinical Decision Support
    • Evidence-based treatment recommendations
    • Contraindication detection across medications
    • Symptom-disease-treatment mapping
  • Patient Journey Modeling
    • Longitudinal health records with semantic context
    • Comorbidity analysis across conditions
    • Treatment outcome prediction

Case Study: A pharmaceutical company implemented a knowledge graph connecting research data, clinical trials, and scientific literature. They identified a novel application for an existing compound, reducing drug development time by 35% and saving $25M in research costs.
